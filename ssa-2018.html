SSA 2018

DHAKA AWAITS TO HOST YOU!

Shape south asia is a 4-day summit that brings together world economic forum global shapers from around the world. Under the theme of inclusion, the summit will portray bangladesh’s challenges and success stories in the areas of poverty alleviation, low-cost healthcare innovation, financial inclusion and sustainable manufacturing.

The summit aims to connect delegates with the tangible networks, capital, and resources necessary to magnify their impacts in these areas.
